My name is Alyssa Topete, a designer, born and raised in the Bay Area who believes in empathic design. My work matches aesthetics with usability, ensuring designs are not only visually appealing but also intuitively navigable. When I’m not immersed in pixels and prototypes, you’ll find me gaming, creating art for streamers or lost in the pages of a good book.

My UX Skills

User Research

Having a empathic and curious nature, I provide valuable insights into the needs, preferences and behaviors our target audience. Understanding their pain points and feedback is the most important to me because of ensuring the users’ satisfaction and overall success.

User Interface

Providing easy and effective UI designs enhances the usability and minimizes the errors of user experiences. I love creating aesthetic user friendly UIs to not only enhance engagement but satisfaction.

Collaboration

Communication, sharing ideas, and designing with teammates is important because of the diverse perspectives we obtain resulting in more innovative solutions.
